Project 1

Our Cpre 288 final project required our group to program the iRobot Create, guiding it through an obstacle course from a randomly assigned "landing point" to a retrieval zone within a specified time frame. The challenge involved avoiding collisions with tall objects, steering clear of floor holes, staying within the course boundaries defined by IR beams, and accomplishing all this without relying on visual confirmation of the robot's location on the obstacle course..
